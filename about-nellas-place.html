Who We Are

Clinically informed care in a stable, loving home

Nella’s Place is a group home in the Cincinnati metro area providing a continuum of treatment, evaluation, and services for girls ages 8 to 21 with emotional and behavioral needs.

Our Mission

Mend mind, body, and spirit

We provide a safe, nurturing environment where girls build routines, restore confidence, and practice life skills that prepare them for school, work, family, and independent living.

What We Do

Residents receive reparative therapy intended to mend the mind, body, and spirit.
