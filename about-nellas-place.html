Who We Are

Clinically informed care in a stable, loving home

Nella’s Place provides clinically informed care in a stable, loving home. We serve girls ages 8 to 21 with individualized treatment, evaluation, and supports.

Our Mission

Mend mind, body, and spirit

Mend mind, body, and spirit through safe routines, restorative relationships, and practice so every resident can thrive at school, work, and home.

What We Do

Residents receive reparative therapy intended to mend the mind, body, and spirit.
