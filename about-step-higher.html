Who We Are

Step Higher is a student success initiative elevating college and career access through tours, scholarships, and mentorship. We partner with families, schools, and community organizations to remove barriers and open doors.

Our Mission

Equip students to see farther and aim higher through exposure, preparation, and sustained support from the first tour to move in day and beyond.
