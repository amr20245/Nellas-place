Nella’s Place Earns 3‑Year CARF Accreditation

We’re thrilled to announce that Nella’s Place has earned a prestigious three‑year accreditation from CARF International. This recognition reflects our dedication to quality, safety, and proven outcomes in residential programming for youth.

The accreditation process involved an intensive site review that examined everything from treatment plans to staff credentials. Achieving a three‑year term—the longest CARF grants—speaks to our team’s passion and professionalism.

Families and community partners can trust that our programs meet rigorous standards. We remain committed to continuous improvement and innovation in our care. Learn more about our approach on the Nella’s Place page.
