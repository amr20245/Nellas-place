Our Story

Nella’s Place is a CARF‑accredited group home for girls ages 8–21. We provide a safe, structured environment with individualized treatment plans, education support, and life‑skills coaching. Our aim is stability today and a pathway to independence tomorrow.

  • On‑site behavioral health services
  • Education & career planning
  • Aftercare & community transitions
