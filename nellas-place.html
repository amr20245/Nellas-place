Our Story

Nella's Place is a CARF accredited group home for girls ages 8 to 21. We provide a safe, structured environment with individualized treatment plans, education support, and life skills coaching. Our goal is stability today and a clear path to independence tomorrow.

  • On site behavioral health services
  • Education and career planning
  • Aftercare and community transitions
