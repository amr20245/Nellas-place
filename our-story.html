Two missions • One organization

Our Story

SHNP unites Step Higher—our college tour and readiness program—with Nella’s Place—our CARF-accredited group home for girls—so youth can see, plan, and step into a bigger future.

Step Higher begins

Born from a simple belief: exposure changes trajectories. Our college tours introduce high school students— especially foster and low‑income youth—to campuses, programs, and mentors while building confidence and a plan for what comes after graduation.

Nella’s Place expands the support

For girls ages 8–21 who need a structured and caring home, Nella’s Place provides a continuum of services— evaluation, treatment, education support, and life skills—so young people can heal and grow.

Accredited by CARF. Learn more →

One pathway, two lanes

Together, Step Higher and Nella’s Place create a pipeline from stability to opportunity— from safe housing and behavioral health to tours, scholarships, and post‑secondary plans.
