Two missions • One organization

Our Story

SHNP brings together Step Higher, our college tour and readiness program, with Nella’s Place, our CARF accredited group home for girls. Youth can see, plan, and step into a bigger future.

Step Higher begins

Born from a simple belief: exposure changes trajectories. Our college tours introduce high school students, especially foster and low income youth, to campuses, programs, and mentors while building confidence and a plan for life after graduation.

Nella’s Place expands the support

For girls ages 8 to 21 who need a structured and caring home, Nella’s Place provides a continuum of services, including evaluation, treatment, education support, and life skills so young people can heal and grow.

CARF accredited. Learn more →

One pathway, two lanes

Together, Step Higher and Nella’s Place create a pipeline from stability to opportunity, from safe housing and behavioral health to tours, scholarships, and postsecondary plans.

Who We Are

Nella’s Place is a Group Home in the Cincinnati, Ohio metro area that provides a continuum of treatment, evaluation, and services for females ages 8 to 21 with emotional and behavioral challenges, referred by the Ohio Department of Jobs and Family Services and other social service organizations.

Our Solid Mission

We provide a safe, nurturing environment for girls mending their mind, body, and spirit.

What We Do
Services Provided

Residents receive reparative therapy services intended to mend minds, bodies, and spirits. Family therapy is provided as needed.
