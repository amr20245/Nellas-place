Service Day at Central State University

Our scholars rolled up their sleeves and spent the afternoon revitalising the community garden at Central State University. From prepping beds to planting perennials, students learned how small acts of service make a big impact on campus culture.

In addition to gardening, participants attended a workshop on sustainability and met with campus leaders to discuss the importance of community involvement. Everyone left with a renewed sense of purpose and connection to the campus.

If you’d like to join us on our next service outing, check out the events page for upcoming opportunities.
